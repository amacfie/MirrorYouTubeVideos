YouTube HTML5 Video Looping Bookmarklet

About

This is a bookmarklet that you can run on any YouTube page. It gives you the ability to indefinitely loop/repeat the whole video or any video segment.

How to use

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar:

Loop video

Use the window that pops up to control the looping action. If you close the window, your settings will stay. You can open the window again by running the bookmarklet.
